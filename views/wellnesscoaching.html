Wellness Coaching

Wellness Coaching offers you the opportunity to create health and wellbeing that works for you. We will have a conversation to explore where you are now with your health and wellbeing, where you would like to be and build a personal plan for you to get there.

The session is led by you and what you would like to discuss, some common topics are:

  • Stress
  • Sleep
  • Relaxation
  • Eating habits
  • Fitness levels
