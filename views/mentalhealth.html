Mental Health

1 in 6 people are currently experiencing mental health difficulties. TM Wellbeing offers mental health and wellbeing support sessions, you’ll have the opportunity to talk through your thoughts and feelings in a supportive setting, we can explore your experiences and discover different techniques to help you manage your mental health.

Session are tailored to you and your needs, you may want to discuss:

  • Stress
  • Anxiety
  • Depression

Appointments

You will be offered an initial appointment of up to 30 minutes, at no cost, this will be an opportunity to meet me, find out more information and discuss how the sessions can support you. The initial appointment is a chance for you to find out if this support is right for you, there is no obligation.

Following your initial appointment, sessions will last for up to one hour. You can book individual or blocks of sessions. Sessions are on a one-to- one basis and can be offered through face-to- face, online or telephone appointments. Face-to- face sessions are held at my home office, based in South Petherton. Alternative venues available on request.

Working from a home office I try to create a cozy and informal atmosphere, it’s important for you to feel relaxed and comfortable. I do have two cats and they sometimes choose to join me for sessions, please let me know if you would rather they stayed out of the way.
